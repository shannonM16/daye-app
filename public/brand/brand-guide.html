06 — Usage

Do's and don'ts

Always use the wordmark or lockup at a minimum size of 80px wide to preserve legibility of the italic letterforms.

Never recolour the logo. Use only the provided dark, light, or lavender variants. Do not apply custom tints, gradients, or drop shadows.

Never stretch or skew the logo. Scale proportionally and preserve the original aspect ratio at all times.

Maintain a clear space equal to the height of the letter "d" on all sides of the logo. Nothing should encroach on this zone.

Use the lavender icon exclusively for social profile pictures. On all other surfaces use dark or light variants.

Cormorant Garamond should only appear in italic. Never use it in roman (upright) weight — the italic is fundamental to the brand voice.

The brand name is always lowercase: daye — never "Daye" or "DAYE" in any logo or product context.
